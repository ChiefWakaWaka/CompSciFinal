Creature specs:
- Speed: idk we'll find something for this
- Health: How much a creature can take from food fighting back
- Energy: Amount of food a creature can get. If at zero the creature dies
- Strength: Attack strength of creature, dictates whether creature will be able to kill food
- Efficiency: Dictates how much energy is used when hunting for food
- RepoChance: Chance that a creature will duplicate
- Lifetime: Value that increases health removed over time to force new generation

Food specs:
- Health: Amount of damage a food can take
- Strength: Amount of damaage a food can gives
